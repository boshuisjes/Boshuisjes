Welcome to Boshuisjes

A forest escape,
thoughtfully made

Nestled in the Wielerbaan Holiday Park on the wooded edge of Wageningen — a vibrant university city in the heart of Gelderland — Boshuisjes is a pair of privately owned forest cottages designed to give you exactly what modern life rarely offers: genuine quiet.

Both cottages are warm, well-equipped and tastefully furnished. Step outside and you're surrounded by ancient oak. Linger on the terrace with coffee, follow a trail, or simply do nothing. The forest takes care of the rest.
