House rules

Clear rules,
simply stated

These rules exist to keep the cottages in good condition for everyone. They're the same for both Boshuisje and Chez-Michel.

🕓

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check-in from 16:00 via lockbox
  • Check-out is by 10:00 — please leave the cottage tidy
  • Early check-in or late check-out may be possible — ask in advance
  • Lockbox code is sent in advance by your hosts
  • No need to coordinate arrival — details are sent in advance./li>
🚭

No Smoking

  • Smoking is strictly prohibited inside both cottages
  • Includes cigarettes, cigars and vaping
  • Smoking outdoors on the terrace is permitted
  • Violation will result in additional cleaning charges

Note: This is actively enforced. Evidence of indoor smoking results in a professional deep-clean charge and may end the stay early.

🐾

Pets Welcome

  • Pets welcome on request — ask before booking
  • A pet surcharge applies — details provided on request
  • Pets must not be left unattended in the cottage
  • Please clean up after your pet in the garden and park
  • Any damage caused by pets is the guest's responsibility
  • Wielerbaan park has a designated dog walking area
👥

Guests & Occupancy

  • Maximum 4 guests per cottage
  • Only registered guests may stay overnight
  • Day visitors are welcome but must not exceed park capacity rules
  • Subletting or hosting additional unregistered guests is not permitted
  • Children are very welcome — high chair and travel cot on request
🌙

Noise & Respect

  • Quiet hours are from 22:00 to 08:00
  • Please respect neighbouring cottages and park residents
  • Music and outdoor gatherings should remain at a considerate volume
  • The park is a nature reserve — please treat it with care
  • Campfires and BBQs outside designated areas are not permitted
🏡

Caring for the Cottage

  • Leave the cottage reasonably tidy on departure
  • Wash and put away all used dishes and utensils
  • Remove all personal belongings and food from the fridge
  • Report any damage — accidents happen and are handled sensibly
  • Do not move furniture or make alterations to the property
  • Dispose of rubbish in the park's designated waste area
🛏️

What's Included

  • Bed linens and towels — one set per guest
  • Additional linen sets available for €25 per set
  • Coffee, tea, and basic cleaning supplies provided
  • Seasonal pool passes left in the cottage (1 per guest)
  • Pool pass replacement: €10 per pass
  • Park keycard provided — replacement: €50
🌳

Park Rules (TopParken)

  • Speed limit within the park: walking pace only
  • Park barrier access via the keycard provided
  • Swimming pool open seasonally — children must be supervised
  • Electric bike charging points are available in the park
  • The park is shared with many families — please be considerate
  • Full park rules available at the reception on Zoomweg 7–9
Booking & Cancellation

Policies

Cancellation Policy

Platform-specific terms may vary — check your booking confirmation. For direct bookings:

More than 30 days before arrival: Full refund.

14–30 days before arrival: 50% refund.

Less than 14 days before arrival: No refund, unless the cottage can be re-let for the same period.

If circumstances change, contact us early — we're flexible when we can be.

Privacy & Data

We collect only what's needed to manage your booking — name, contact details, and guest numbers. Nothing else.

Data is not shared with third parties except the booking platform you used.

Stored securely and deleted after one year. Request deletion at any time.

Liability

Boshuisjes and its hosts are not liable for personal injury, loss, or damage to guests' belongings during their stay, except where caused by negligence on the part of the hosts.

Guests are responsible for their own safety and the safety of their party, including children and pets, within the cottage and across the park.
