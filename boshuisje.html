Boshuisje A06

A cottage that feels
genuinely private

Boshuisje sits in the quieter, tree-lined A-section of Wielerbaan park — one of two privately owned cottages that Christopher and Zuzana have been carefully maintaining and improving for years. The oak forest starts at the edge of the terrace. The nearest neighbour is far enough away to matter.

Inside, it's warm and practical without being fussy. Dark kitchen cabinets, a proper dining table for four, a comfortable sitting area, and a bedroom that can be made up as a double or as twin singles — just mention your preference when you book. Everything you need for a week is here: full kitchen, Senseo machine, linens and towels included.

It works equally well for couples wanting a quiet week, families with young children, or researchers and professionals who need a comfortable, well-located base near Wageningen University. The park is calm, the forest is on your doorstep, and the city centre is six minutes by car.

Bedroom

One bedroom with a double bed — or configured as two singles if you prefer. Built-in storage, blackout curtains, and a window that looks into the trees. Bed linens included; extra sets available for €25.

Living & dining room

Open-plan sitting and dining area with a sofa, flat-screen TV, and a solid wooden dining table for four. Comfortable without being overdone. The side window faces the forest; the front looks out to the private terrace.

Kitchen

A proper kitchen — not a kitchenette. Gas hob, microwave, full-size fridge, Senseo coffee machine, and everything you need to cook a real meal. Crockery, glassware, pots and utensils all included.

Terrace & garden

Private terrace with outdoor furniture. The garden extends into the forest edge — oak trees on three sides. It's the kind of place where mornings stretch. A fire pit is available on request.
