About this cottage

Boshuisje (A06) is a privately owned and managed forest cottage nestled in the A-section of the Wielerbaan Holiday Park in Wageningen. With space for up to 4 guests, it offers a flexible and comfortable retreat — whether you're a couple seeking quiet, a small family after space, or visiting researchers needing a proper home base.

The bedroom can be configured as one double bed or two singles — just let us know your preference in advance. Step outside and the oak forest begins immediately. Sit on the terrace with coffee and let the morning happen at its own pace.
