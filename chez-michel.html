Chez-Michel

A character-filled forest cottage for up to four guests — Chez-Michel is the more spacious of our two cottages, with two proper bedrooms, a generous open-plan living and dining area, and panoramic bay windows that look directly onto the garden and the surrounding oak trees. Decorated with care over many years, our guests consistently call it the most relaxing place they've ever stayed.

The large bay windows flood the cottage with natural light whatever the season. Patio doors in the living room open directly onto the private garden terrace — perfect for morning coffee under the trees, or a long summer evening with a glass of wine.

The rooms

Master bedroom — double/twin

A double bed with built-in overhead storage and warm reading lights. Forest views from the window. Linens and towels included (extras €25/set).

Second bedroom — bunks + workspace

A cheerful yellow bunk room sleeping two, with a full-width built-in desk, ergonomic chair and overhead storage. Perfect for children, or equally for a researcher or remote worker who needs a proper workspace away from the living area.

Living & dining room

Airy open-plan space with leather sofas, dining table for four, flat-screen TV, and the striking red-umbrella painting guests always remark on. Bay windows wrap the front of the cottage. Patio doors open to the terrace.

Kitchen

Full dark-cabinet kitchen with gas hob, microwave, full-size fridge, Senseo coffee maker, and the beautiful oak china cabinet fully stocked with crockery and glasses. The forest window makes everything feel less like a chore.

Bathroom

Complete bathroom with walk-in shower, heated towel rail, WC and sink. Fresh towels included.
