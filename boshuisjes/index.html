Welcome to Boshuisjes

A forest escape,
thoughtfully made

Nestled in the Wielerbaan Holiday Park on the wooded edge of Wageningen — a vibrant university city in the heart of Gelderland — Boshuisjes is a pair of privately owned forest cottages designed to give you exactly what modern life rarely offers: genuine quiet.

Both cottages are warm, well-equipped and tastefully furnished with everything you need to feel completely at home. Step outside and you're surrounded by ancient oak. Linger on the terrace with coffee, follow a trail through the trees, or simply do nothing at all. The forest takes care of the rest.

The Surroundings

Wageningen —
more than you expect

Often overlooked, always loved by those who discover it. Wageningen is a vibrant green university city surrounded by some of the most beautiful cycling and walking terrain in the Netherlands.

🚴

World-Class Cycling

The Veluwe and Rhine riverbanks are on your doorstep. Hundreds of kilometres of cycling paths wind through forests, dykes, and charming villages. Bike hire is easily arranged locally.

Starting from your front door
🌳

Ancient Oak Forest

The park borders the Wageningse Berg — a rare hilly landscape in the Dutch heartland covered in oak, beech, and heather moorland. Perfect for morning walks with the family or a dog.

0 min walk
🏙️

Wageningen City Centre

A lively university town full of independent cafés, weekly markets, excellent restaurants and a warm local atmosphere. One of those places that feels like a secret not yet discovered.

6 min drive · 10 min by bike
🌿

Belmonte Arboretum

A beautiful botanical garden managed by Wageningen University, filled with rare trees from around the world. Extraordinary in autumn colour, magical in spring blossom.

10 min drive
🛶

The Rhine & Grebbeberg

Follow the Rhine for scenic walks and kayaking, or explore the historic Grebbeberg — a landscape layered with natural beauty and wartime history that still moves people deeply.

15 min drive
🛒

Everyday Essentials

The nearest supermarket is minutes away. Wageningen's weekly market is great for fresh Dutch produce, local cheeses, and flowers. Everything you need, effortlessly close.

3 min drive to supermarket

Common Questions

Everything you
need to know

What are the check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is from 16:00 via self check-in lockbox — no need to wait for anyone. Check-out is by 10:00. If you need flexibility, just contact us in advance and we'll do our best.
Is there free parking?
Yes — each cottage has its own private parking space, completely free. The park has a barrier; a keycard is provided inside the cottage for access. Note: TopParken charges €50 to replace a lost card.
What's included in the rate?
Bed linens and towels (one set per guest), Wi-Fi, cable TV, tea and coffee, soap and cleaning supplies, free parking, and seasonal swimming pool passes. Additional linen sets available on request for €25 per set.
Are the cottages family-friendly?
Absolutely. Both cottages are ideal for families with children. A high chair and travel cot are available on request at no extra charge. The forest setting is safe, peaceful and magical for kids.
Can we book both cottages at once?
Yes! Both can be booked simultaneously — perfect for two families or a larger group wanting adjacent but private spaces in the same forest setting.
Is there a swimming pool?
Yes — the Wielerbaan park has a seasonal pool. Pool passes are left in the cottage, ready for your arrival. Please return them before departure (TopParken charges €10 per replacement).
How far is Wageningen University (WUR)?
Approximately 6 minutes by car or 15 minutes by bicycle. We regularly host visiting researchers, lecturers, conference delegates, and parents of students. Flexible stays are available.
What's the nearest supermarket?
About 3 minutes by car or 6 minutes by bike. Wageningen city centre — with markets, restaurants and more — is just 6 minutes by car. The park also has a small on-site restaurant.
