The people
behind Boshuisjes

Christopher is Dutch-British, based in the Netherlands, and has been managing the cottages since they were first let. Zuzana is Slovak, speaks four languages fluently, and handles much of the guest communication.

They've hosted guests from over 20 countries — researchers at WUR, families on holiday, couples looking for a quiet week, and everything in between. They know the area well and are genuinely useful when you ask for recommendations.

Their approach is straightforward: available when you need them, out of your way when you don't. Call, WhatsApp, or email — they respond quickly and in English, Dutch, Slovak or Czech.
